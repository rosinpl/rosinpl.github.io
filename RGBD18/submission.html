Author Guidelines

All submissions will be handled electronically through the online submission system. Each paper will be peer-reviewed. Reviews will be double-blind according to ACCV guidelines. In submitting a manuscript to this workshop, the authors acknowledge that during the review period no paper substantially similar in content has been submitted to another workshop or conference.

Paper Length and Format

Manuscripts should be submitted as PDF files. The submission page length is 14 pages for content plus maximum 2 pages for references.

Paper Style and Template

The paper template is identical to the main ACCV2018 conference: LaTeX Templates (zip): accv2018kit.zip
Example submission paper with detailed instructions: accv2018submission.pdf

Online Submission Site

Please submit papers by 24th September 2018 through the online submission site.

Registration

To have your paper included in the proceedings at least one of the authors must be registered at a full registration rate.
