Author Guidelines

All submissions will be handled electronically through the online submission system. Each paper will be peer-reviewed. Reviews will be double-blind according to PSIVT guidelines. In submitting a manuscript to this workshop, the authors acknowledge that during the review period no paper substantially similar in content has been submitted to another workshop or conference (except for dual submission to the PSIVT2017 main conference which is allowed; however, only one destination is allowed: if both submissions are accepted, the author must choose either the main conference or the workshop to be published in).

Accepted workshop papers will be published by Springer in the Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series.

Paper Length and Format

Manuscripts should be submitted as PDF files and should be no more than 14 pages in the LNCS paper format.

Paper Style and Template

Articles should be prepared in English according to the Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science guidelines and templates.

Online Submission Site

Please submit papers through the online submission site.

Registration

To have your paper included in the proceedings at least one of the authors must be registered at a full registration rate. According to IAPR rules, one registration is fine for having multiple co-authored papers at the conference.
