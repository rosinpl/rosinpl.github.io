Workshops and Summer Schools 2016

The following are a list of Workshops and Summer Schools that are of potential interest to PhD students in the School of Computer Science & Informatics.

If you have any further suggestions please let me know so that they can be added to the list.

Paul Rosin
PGR Training Coordinator
Paul.Rosin@cs.cf.ac.uk

Conference Link Proposer Their Comments
British Machine Vision Association Computer Vision Summer School http://www.bmva.org/summerschool Paul Rosin Many of our PhD students have gone and found it useful.
Neural Information Processing Systems (NIPS) https://nips.cc/ Humphrey Sheil NIPS is relatively accessible this year travel-wise - it's on in Barcelona instead of east / west coast of North America.
European Summer School in Logic, Language and Information (ESSLLI) http://esslli2016.unibz.it/ Martin Caminada I have also taught myself.
European Agents Systems Summer School (EASSS) http://www.iiia.csic.es/easss2015/ Martin Caminada I have also taught myself.
Computational Visual Media Conference (CVM) http://iccvm.org/2016/ Ralph Martin CVM will be held in Cardiff this year.
European Summer School in Logic, Language and Information (ESSLLI) http://esslli2016.unibz.it/ Irena Spasic
International School on Foundations of Security Analysis and Design (FOSAD) http://www.sti.uniurb.it/events/fosad/ George Theodorakopoulos I was there myself as a PhD student, and it usually attracts quite good lecturers. The total fees are around 900 Euros and they have some funding for PhD students. The application deadline is 20 June 2016 (not open yet).
WSTNet Web Science Summer School (WWSSS) http://wwsss16.west.uni-koblenz.de/ Pete Burnap This is a workshop for those interested in Web research and Social Computing. It focuses on how the Web is built, managed, used and governed; and will include the study of human behaviour, spread of information and data management.
IACR Summer School IACR Summer School Blockchain Technologies https://bitcoinschool.gr/ George Theodorakopoulos A summer school on Bitcoin and cryptography.
